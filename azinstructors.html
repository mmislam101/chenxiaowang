Betty Dong has been studying Taijiquan since 1987 and has been teaching since 1989.

In November 2007, she was awarded a 5th Duan Wei certificate from the Chinese Wushu Association after examination. In April 2011, she was awarded a 6th Duan Wei certificate.

Chris Forde has been studying Taijiquan since 1992 and has been teaching since 2001.

Betty and Chris have been students of Grandmaster Chen Xiaowang since 1997 and were both inducted as his disciples in March 2006. They host Master Chen at their homes during his visits to Arizona and frequently travel in the U.S., Europe, and China to study with him.
